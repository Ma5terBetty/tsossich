About Me

Commerce Manager and Indie Game Developer

I’m a passionate game designer dedicated to creating immersive experiences. Over the past six years, I’ve been honing my skills as a technical game developer at the Universidad Argentina de la Empresa.

I thrive in collaborative teams where there’s a strong sense of belonging. I value listening to others and embracing diverse perspectives to enhance the project. I believe the journey of learning and refining my craft as a game designer is ongoing and ever-evolving.

I also identify with the design philosophy of Jesse Schell, particularly his focus on creating experiences where carefully crafted stimuli evoke specific emotions to achieve a desired outcome.

Knowlegde and Tools

Unity

My academic focus on C# programming led me to embrace Unity as my primary development engine. Through my studies, I gained a comprehensive understanding of object-oriented programming (OOP) and design patterns.

Unreal Engine

I’ve developed a solid foundation in Unreal Engine, learning blueprint programming, level design, asset management, and prototyping.

Adobe Photoshop

Starting as a hobbyist, I explored photo editing and image retouching, which evolved into creating seamless textures and pixel art for my games. Photoshop has also become an essential tool in my design process.

Adobe Illustrator

Primarily used for crafting 2D elements like sprites and UI components, Illustrator also serves as a versatile tool for creating precise level design assets and planning layouts for my games.

Asset Forge

Its intuitive interface and functionality make it a go-to tool for rapid prototyping and testing in my game development workflow.

Amplify Shader Editor

Building on my understanding of shaders and post-processing, I’ve used Amplify Shader Editor to create customized shaders, giving me full creative control over visual effects and materials in my projects.
